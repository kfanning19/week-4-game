CrystalCollector

The Rules

At the beginning of the game, you will be assigned a random target score to meet. Clicking on the crystals below will add to your score. Each is worth a different amount. To win the game, you must reach your target score exactly. If you go over, you lose. The value of each crystal won't be revealed until you click on it, and they will change at the beginning of each game.
